Tim Berners-Lee

Tim Berners-Lee is a British computer scientist and the inventor of the World Wide Web. He created the first
web browser and the foundational technologies of the web, including HTML, HTTP, and URLs. His work made
it possible to share information globally through the internet. Berners-Lee has dedicated much of his career
to promoting an open and accessible web for everyone.
