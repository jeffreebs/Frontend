Music instruments

Music is a universal form of expression that connects people beyond language and culture. It helps convey
emotions, tell stories, and strengthen cultural identity. Additionally, it has a positive impact on emotional
well-being and cognitive development. It can reduce stress, improve mood, and enhance creativity. Music
also plays an important role in education and social connection.

Guitar

The guitar is a very popular instrument used in many music genres such as rock, pop, and classical music. It
can be used for both accompaniment and solo performances. It is valued for its versatility and portability.

  • Type: String
  • Main material: Wood and nylon or metal strings
  • Common use: Solo and accompaniment

The Bear

The piano is a fundamental instrument in both classical and modern music. It allows musicians to play
melody and harmony at the same time, making it very versatile. It is widely used for composition and music education

  • Type: String (percussive strings)
  • Main material: Wood, metal, and strings
  • Common use: Solo and accompaniment

The Shark

Drums are the rhythmic foundation of many music styles such as rock, jazz, and pop. They consist of several
drums and cymbals played together. Drums are essential for keeping time and rhythm.

  • Type: Percussion
  • Main material: Wood, metal, and plastic
  • Common use: Rhythmic accompaniment
