The Kings of the World

The earth has many different animals around the world, but in specific places, some animals are considered kings, in your territory they have the control, we will speak about the Bear, Lion and Shark

The Lion

The lion is a powerful wild animal known as the king of the jungle.
It lives mainly in Africa and is recognized for its strength, courage, and social behavior in groups called prides.

  • Weight: 150–250 kg
  • Length: 1.7–2.5 m
  • Main food sources: Large herbivores
  • Life expectancy: 10–20 years

The Bear

The bear is a large and powerful wild animal found in different parts of the world.
It is known for its strength, intelligence, and ability to adapt to many environments.

  • Weight: 100–600 kg
  • Length: 1.5–3 m
  • Main food sources: Fish, fruits, plants, insects, and small animals
  • Life expectancy: 20–30 years

The Shark

The shark is a powerful marine animal that lives in oceans around the world.
It is known for its sharp senses, speed, and role as a top predator in the sea.

  • Weight: 600-1100 kg
  • Length: 0.5- 6 m
  • Main food sources: Fish, seals, squid, and other marine animals
  • Life expectancy: 20–30 years
