The 5 best blog sites in 2023

Choose the best blogging platform to showcase your content.

By Jeffree Berrocal

Blogging is far from dead. As we enter 12 months of February—sorry, X—armies have discovered a brand new platform to sound your thoughts off about issues online. You want a platform where you can share your thoughts properly and keep control of things, it's impossible to beat a blog. Plus, you can always share your blog posts on social media.

Promote and publish your content with automation Similarly, if you run a business and want to provide resources and recommendations to customers, the best way to do it is often to add a blog to your website. Best of all, your blog content gets indexed by Google—unlike almost all social media posts—so you can drive potential customers to your business through content marketing (and without having to pay for ads). Look at the blog you're reading right now: Zapier blog posts get millions of views per month and are one of the most valuable ways of getting new customers at Zapier.

  • WordPress - The most popular blogging platform
  • Medium - Simple and clean writing experience
  • Wix - Easy drag-and-drop website builder
  • Ghost - Modern publishing platform
  • Substack - Newsletter-first blogging
