Social Media at 2026

Social media are tools used to communicate with other people. They are a way to meet others, see content, and talk. There are many different social media platforms, but all of them are used for
information and entertainment. Many people can watch videos and also communicate with others.
Companies use social media daily to connect with customers, show products and services, and sell.
However, some users simply enjoy spending a small part of their day
consuming content on social media.
